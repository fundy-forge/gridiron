Getting Here

On The River

Twenty-five minutes from uptown Saint John, right where Main Street bends to meet the water. Easy to find, easier to stay.

Map — 14 Riverside Lane

By Car

Free lot off Riverside Lane, plus street parking along Main. Designated-driver soft drinks always on the house.

On Foot & Bike

Right on the Hampton trail network. Bike racks out front, and the river boardwalk runs to our patio gate.

Good To Know

Dog & kid friendly · wheelchair accessible · cards only · 19+ after 8 PM. No reservations — just turn up.
